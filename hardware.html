FlatOffice-Stick       

The FlatOffice-Stick is a safe, easy and sustainable USB-Stick. Prepared for secure connecting employees and protecting company interests.

Hardware Features

Safe, easy and sustainable

Hardware and Firmware protected against manipulation

Fully encrypted firmware and user data

Removable storage media slot for user data

Additional USB-output (no loss of USB-Slots on the device)

External power-supply possible

Recycable, upgradable and reuseable

Ultra fast USB 3.x support
