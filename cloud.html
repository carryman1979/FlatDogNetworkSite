FlatCompany          

is your virtual company in a cloud service. In 4 steps configured. Connects your employees easily.

Cloud Features

your virtual company ready in 4 steps 

01.

Register

1. Goto https://www.flatdog.net/register.html
2. Choose "Register a new FlatCompany"
3. Fill registration form
4. Order Master-FlatOffice Sticks 

02.

Prepare

1. Add an organisation chart of your company
2. Add existing roles in your company
3. Add your employees
4. Add applications and widgets needed to do the work in your company

03.

Configure

1. Link the employees with your organization chart units
2. Link roles to your employees
3. Connect applications and widgets to organization units
4. Connect applications and widgets to roles
5. Or connect applications and widgets to employees direct

04.

Order

Order FlatOffice-Sticks for each FlatCompany member

...

Keep up-to-date

Repeat step 02 till 04 if something changes
