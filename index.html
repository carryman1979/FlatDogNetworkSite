The FlatOffice

A whole office at a little USB-Stick. Sounds unbelievable? That is it. But exactly that is what our FlatOffice-Stick stands for: Carry all you need for your work in your pocket, with our clever mobile-office solution.
From working time recording to break time fun apps - the possibilities are nearly endless.
Security is the top priority: With our patented manipulation protection and many more established security systems are your data secured to 100%.
Even our nature environment is very important to us: Beside the CO² reducing effect by renouncing driving to the working place, the USB-stick is nearby fully developed with sustainable materials.
